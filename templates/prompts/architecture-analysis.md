# Architecture Analysis

Analyze this system's architecture:
1. **Components** — what are the major pieces and their responsibilities?
2. **Data flow** — how does data move through the system?
3. **Dependencies** — what depends on what? Where are the coupling points?
4. **Failure modes** — what breaks when each component fails?
5. **Scaling limits** — where will this hit walls?

Be specific. Reference actual code/config, not hypotheticals.
